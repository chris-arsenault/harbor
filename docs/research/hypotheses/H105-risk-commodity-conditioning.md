# H105 — Risk-on/risk-off and commodity conditioning

- Status: proposed / external data layer needed

## Hypothesis

JPY and AUD pairs should be conditioned on risk appetite and commodity state:
JPY strengthens in risk-off, AUD behaves like a risk/commodity currency.

## Data requirement

SPX/VIX/gold/oil daily series at minimum. Best used as a filter/conditioning
layer rather than a standalone trigger.
